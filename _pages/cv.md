Professionnal Profile
======
I am a biological oceanographer with a Ph.D. and a total of five years of experience both in the field and in laboratories. My skills includes programing, methods design and management, data collection in ecology and physical oceanography, science communication. I have experience in translating coastal and biological data into relevant outputs for partners and stakeholders. I have the capacity to lead and be a part of multi-disciplinary field operations, to build partnerships, and communicate my findings to specialist and non-specialist audiences alike.
