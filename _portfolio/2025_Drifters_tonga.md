Drifters data were collected by [Oceanly Science Ltd](https://oceanlyscience.com/#) under Inkfish, and I was tasked with general QAQC and analysis including particles projections. The particles projections were done using [parcels](https://parcels-code.org/) and the North & Eastward [current velocities](https://doi.org/10.48670/moi-00016) and [winds](https://doi.org/10.48670/moi-00305), both datasets available on [Copernicus](https://data.marine.copernicus.eu/products?disc=facets). The QAQC inlcuded flagging eroneous locations, anomalous speed, kinetic energy and eddy kinetic energy using formulas from [Siji and Pattiaratchi, (2025)](https://www.mdpi.com/2077-1312/13/4/717#).
